From January 2019 to June 2020, Jay worked for Schuff Steel, the nation’s largest steel fabricator. Working in the firm’s main Phoenix shop, he served as a welder/fitter. Jay earned multiple welding and crane certifications that allowed him to work on over 35 major fabrication projects. With the assistance of blue prints and other technical guides, he helped to fabricate hospitals, stadiums, hotels, bridges, among other edifices.
