At the age of eight, Jay began playing drums. As an adolescent, he played in several bands throughout Orange County, California. He would later go on to learn to play guitar, bass, and piano. Throughout high school, Jay earned money by DJing at weddings and parties. In 2012, he started a project called FIXIE, a EDM-driven music project that combined his love for music and knowledge of technical music production. In 2014, FIXIE landed a DJ residency at the JB Nightclub in Portland which lasted for three months. FIXIE has performed on the radio (KXLU 88.9 FM) and throughout venues in California and Oregon.
